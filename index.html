Book Tennis Court

STEP 1

Enter the following link:

Google Calendar

And then click the + icon (highlighted in red, step 1).

STEP 2

You will be redirected to another page, where you should click the ADD button (highlighted in red, step 2).

STEP 3

You need to select the day and time to book the court in the calendar (highlighted in red, step 3).

STEP 4

You will see a new tab:

  • Add a title (highlighted in red, step 4').
  • Add torrenueva.tennis@gmail.com as a guest (highlighted in red, step 4'').
  • Save the event (highlighted in red, step 4''').

STEP 5

Click the SEND button (highlighted in red, step 5).

Then you will see the court booked in the calendar.

STEP 6

If you want to cancel the booking, you will see the option in the calendar.
